# 💸 Module 05: Fintech AI

> **Moving from "talking bots" to "autonomous financial agents."**

In the finance industry, the "fancy" terms shift away from general tech and toward risk, compliance, money movement, and customer intelligence. As of 2026, the buzz is all about moving from "talking bots" to "autonomous financial agents."

## 💡 The 2024 vs. 2026 Shift

| 2024 (Experimental) | 2026 (Operational) |
|---------------------|--------------------|
| Chatbots (answering questions) | Autonomous Agents (paying bills) |
| Manual Audits (checking samples) | Continuous Monitoring (checking 100% of data) |
| Basic Fraud Alerts (notifying you) | Predictive Protection (blocking before it happens) |
