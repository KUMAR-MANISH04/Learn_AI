# 🚀 Module 04: Industry Terminology AI (The 2026 Shift)

> **Welcome to the 2026 Shift. We are moving away from "Generation" (making text) and toward "Reasoning & Execution" (solving problems).**

This module covers the language being used in "AI-Native" companies and advanced engineering teams right now to solve the problems that basic chatbots can't handle. If you use terms like "Agentic Workflows," "MCP integrations," and "Graph-based RAG" in the same sentence, you’ll sound like you're working at a top-tier AI lab.
